About me


I am Mahyar Kantoori

I was born in 1989 in the city of silver rains in Iran "Rasht". I am a photographer. For me, photography is not just recording the image, but feeling what I want to show to my audience, sometimes I sew the pristine and naked face of nature to your eyes, and sometimes the image of people living their true selves.

During my time as a professional photographer, I organized a group photo exhibition called "Dia" which was a combination of photos and poetry and I achieved the results that you'll read in the following:

  • The statue of the nature section of the Bandar Anzali Film and Photo Festival.
  • Diploma of honor in the emotional portrait section of the thirty-fifth awards festival of Russia.
  • Diploma of honor in the birds section of the thirty-fifth awards festival of Russia.
  • Appreciation of the jury of the Nature Festival Kanon Persia Rasht.
  • Diploma of honor in the food photography section of the thirty-fifth awards festival of Russia.
